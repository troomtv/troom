About Us

Our Story

Founded in 2023, Teaching Room, popularly known as TRoom was born out of a passion for helping young men and women to understand the love of God and to uphold the principles concerned with walking with God. What started as a small startup in Ghana, has now become a space for all young adults across the globe to know God more. We believe in meaningful partnerships, continuous learning, and wisdom-power demonstration.

Our Mission

To create a space for growth among young people. Growth, not only in spiritual understandings but also in physical matters.

Our Vision

To develop young men and women into vessels of honor unto God.
